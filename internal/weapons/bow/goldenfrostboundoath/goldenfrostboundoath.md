# Weapons

## Golden Frostbound Oath (ID:90661 / JP:霜契の金枝)
Bow
LV 1 Stats: Base ATK 41 / CRIT DMG 19.2%

Increase DEF by 16%/20%/24%/28%/32%. When the equipping character's Elemental Skill or Lunar-Crystallize attacks hit opponents, gain the Frost Fae's Favor effect for 6s: Geo DMG inflicted by the equipping character increases by 40%/50%/60%/70%/80%, Lunar-Crystallize Reaction DMG increases by 40%/50%/60%/70%/80%. While this effect is active, if there are Moondrifts near the equipping character, all other nearby party members will gain the Frost Fae's Mischief effect: Geo DMG dealt increases by 20%/25%/30%/35%/40% and Lunar-Crystallize Reaction DMG increases by 20%/25%/30%/35%/40%. This effect can be triggered even when the equipping character is off-field.
